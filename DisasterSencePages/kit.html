Storage Tips

  • 🏠 Store kit in easily accessible location known to all family members
  • 🎒 Use waterproof containers and bags to protect items
  • 📍 Keep smaller kits in car, workplace, and school
  • 🌡️ Store in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • 📝 Label everything with contents and expiration dates
  • 🔄 Rotate perishable items regularly
